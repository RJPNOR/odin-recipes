Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Rich flavours of tomato and beef with the delightful texture of pasta, while by no means traditional bolognese this one is still delicous and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef mince
  • 2 Cloves garlic
  • 1 Onion
  • 2tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Tin of chopped tomatos
  • 1 Beef stock cube
  • 1tbsp Italian herbs and seasoning
  • 1tbsp Oregano
  • 1tbsp Basil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1tsp Black pepper
  • Your choice of pasta and amount

Steps

  1. Add the olive oil to a pan and cook the onion on medium high till yellowish and translucent.
  2. Mince the garlic and add to the pan then cook for a further minute.
  3. Add the beef to the pan and make sure your roughly chopping and stiring it with a wooden spoon until it browns.
  4. Add the tin of tomatos to the pan (you can blend it if you dont want chunks of tomato, i personally like them).
  5. Add the beef stock, herbs, salt and pepper then stir.
  6. Let this start to bubble then turn the heat down and leave to simmer for at least 30 mins.
  7. When you are ready to eat bring a pot of water to the boil and cook your pasta.
  8. Plate up the pasta and place however much sauce you like on top, the rest can be frozen for easy meals later.
  9. Serve and enjoy with some optional cheese!
