Chicken Madras

Description

This recipe will have you create a delicous almost takeaway like chicken madras that will take little more than 10 minutes to cook and scratch that friday night curry itch!

Ingredients

  • 1x onion
  • 2x garlic cloves
  • 1x tsp ginger
  • 3x tbsp ghee
  • 3x chicken breast
  • 1/2x tsp ground cinnamon
  • 3x tbsp curry powder
  • 1 & 1/2x tsp paprika
  • 1/4x tsp fenugreek
  • 1x tsp tamarind paste
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1x 400ml tin tomato
  • 2x tbsp tomato puree
  • 1x 400ml tin coconut milk
  • Serve with rice or roti

Steps

  1. To begin start by blending the onion, garlic and ginger into a paste. Melt the ghee in a large pan on high and cook the paste until starting to brown around the edges (roughly 5 mins).
  2. Chop the chicken into chunks and add to the pan, cooking until sealed.
  3. Turn the heat down to medium and add all the spices and seasoning, including the tamarind. Cook this for 1 min until spices are fragrant.
  4. Blend the tinned tomato, add to the pan with the tomato puree and bring to the boil.
  5. Add the coconut milk, stir it in and bring to the boil. Simmer for 10 mins.
  6. Serve with your rice or roti and enjoy!
